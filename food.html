Traditional Foods of Botswana

Seswaa

Slow-cooked beef or goat, Botswana’s national dish.

Bogobe

A thick porridge made from maize meal served with meat or vegetables.

Phane

High protein edible caterpillars that can be dried or cooked.

Dikgobe

Beans cooked together into a hearty meal.

Chapatis

Popular fried dough snack.

Serobe

A dish made from cow or goat intestines and tripe.
