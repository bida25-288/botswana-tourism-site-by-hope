Traditional Foods of Botswana

Seswaa

Slow-cooked beef, a national dish of Botswana.

Bogobe

Traditional porridge made from sorghum or maize.

Morogo

Wild spinach often served with pap.

Vetkoek

Deep-fried dough bread, sometimes filled with mince.

Serobe

Traditional dish made from goat or cow intestines.

Phane

Mopane worms, a popular protein-rich delicacy.

Madila

Sour milk enjoyed traditionally.

Magwinya

Fat cakes, a popular snack.
